Sofia Botti

Postdoctoral Researcher at Università della Svizzera Italiana

About

Modeling cardiac tissue, ionic models for hiPSC-CMs, numerical methods & machine learning for regenerative medicine. Based in Lugano, Switzerland. I develop multi-scale mathematical and numerical models for cardiac tissue that connect ionic currents at the single-cell level to 3D ventricle simulations. My work combines PDE/ODE modeling, high-performance computing and machine learning to advance precision cardiology and regenerative medicine.
